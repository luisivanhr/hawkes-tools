Top-level export parity: every name in the local `tick.hawkes.__all__` is
available from `hawkes_tools.hawkes`. Extra convenience exports are
`TimeFunction` and `HawkesKernel`.

the package imports without `tick`, but it requires Numba and dispatches hot
loops through JIT helpers. Python/NumPy reference formulas remain in-tree for
equivalence assertions. Benchmark smoke coverage reports cold, warm, and direct
reference timings but does not enforce wall-clock thresholds:
